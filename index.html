Our Initiatives

Blackboard Course Speaker Series Fajitas for Finance Telenovela Series

At Enactus UTRGV, we're committed to increasing financial literacy in our community through four key initiatives. These projects are designed to be entrepreneurial, sustainable, and scalable, with the goal of creating lasting positive impact.

Blackboard Course

Online Financial Education

Our Blackboard Course offers comprehensive financial education to UTRGV students in a convenient online format. The course covers essential topics that every college student needs to know about personal finance.

  • Basic budgeting and saving strategies
  • Understanding credit and managing debt
  • Introduction to investing
  • Student loan management
  • Self-paced learning with assessments

"The Blackboard Course helped me understand how to start budgeting while in college. Now I feel more confident about managing my finances."

- UTRGV Student

Students can access this course through their Blackboard account. It includes videos, readings, quizzes, and practical exercises designed to build financial skills.

Speaker Series

Learn from Financial Experts

Our Speaker Series brings financial professionals and experts to campus for informative presentations and Q&A sessions. These events provide students and community members the opportunity to learn from industry leaders and ask questions specific to their situation.

  • Regular events throughout the academic year
  • Diverse range of financial topics covered
  • Networking opportunities with professionals
  • Open to all students and community members
  • Recorded sessions available through our Resources page

"The investment basics presentation helped me understand how to start building wealth even as a student with limited income."

- Attendee

Past speakers have included representatives from State Farm Insurance, local banks, real estate professionals, investment advisors, and successful entrepreneurs.

Fajitas for Finance

Financial Education with a Flavor

Fajitas for Finance brings financial literacy education directly to community organizations in a format that's engaging and accessible. These events combine delicious food with practical financial knowledge in a relaxed setting.

  • Interactive workshops at community locations
  • Content customized to audience needs
  • Bilingual presentation options (English/Spanish)
  • Take-home resources and materials
  • Free meal for all participants

"Our church group learned so much from the Fajitas for Finance workshop. The presenters made complex topics easy to understand, and the food was fantastic!"

- Community Member

Fajitas for Finance is available to community groups, churches, schools, and other organizations throughout the Rio Grande Valley. Our team will work with you to tailor the content to your group's specific needs and interests.

Telenovela Series

Financial Education through Storytelling

Our Telenovela Series presents financial concepts through engaging short videos inspired by the popular Latin American dramatic format. This innovative approach makes financial education more relatable and memorable, especially for Spanish-speaking audiences.

  • Engaging storylines that illustrate financial concepts
  • Available in both English and Spanish
  • Short episodes ideal for social media sharing
  • Covers topics like budgeting, credit, and saving
  • Created by UTRGV students

"I shared the telenovela episodes with my parents who never had formal financial education. They found it entertaining and learned a lot!"

- UTRGV Student

The Telenovela Series transforms complex financial topics into accessible, entertaining content that resonates with viewers of all ages. Each episode includes practical tips that viewers can apply to their own financial situations.
